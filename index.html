3-Axis CNC Styrofoam Cutter

Designed by a team of three to customer specification and used for forming quadcopter bodies of various shapes out of styrofoam. Multiple designs were weighed based on criteria and parts were purchased or 3D printed.

Material Sorter

An electro-mechanical sorting system developed to identify and sort four different types of materials. Ferromagnetic, reflective, optical, and Hall sensors were used to ensure objects were placed in the correct bin with respective signals were sent through low pass filters to optimize accuracy and speed.

Custom PC Build

I've built multiple custom PCs based on budget, specification needs, and price trends. In addition component overclocking is sometimes used to reach performance goals while staying on budget.

Arduino Espresso Machine Concept

A pre-build design of an Arduino microcontrollered espresso machine, pressure and temperature are controlled by a microcontroller to ensure quality espresso. This design is intended to provide an economical and convenient alternative to machines currently available.

Bike Vancouver Website

A website about cycling around Vancouver built for a class taken at BCIT. The site details good rides to try, recommends bike shops around the city, and promotes local cycling events.
